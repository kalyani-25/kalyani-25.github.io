Hi, I’m Kalyani Vaidya 👋

I’m a Software Engineer and AI/ML Researcher passionate about building intelligent, secure, and scalable systems that bridge automation, data, and backend craftsmanship. Currently, I work as a Security Analyst-Software & Automation at Central Michigan University’s SOC, where I design cloud-native automation frameworks with Python, Microsoft Defender 365, Kafka, and Elastic Stack to streamline threat detection and data pipelines.

Before joining CMU, I was a Software Engineer at APRG Technologies (India), where I built enterprise-grade microservices and APIs with Java (Spring Boot), PostgreSQL, and Docker. That’s where I learned that “clean code” isn’t just about syntax it’s about empathy for whoever debugs it at 2 a.m. (often… me). 😅

My journey since then has been a mix of backend design, cloud infrastructure, and applied AI/ML engineering. From building a GAN-based fashion design system that converts sketches into photo-realistic garments 🎨 to developing EcoBeanAI, a predictive model for cocoa and coffee quality, I’ve worked at the intersection of creativity, data, and security.

Outside of Tech

When I’m not coding, I’m usually painting landscapes, reading thrillers & sci-fi, or exploring how many flat whites one person can safely consume in a day (ongoing research ☕). I like balancing logic with creativity and writing code that future me actually enjoys reading.

I’m open to collaboration in AI engineering, cloud development, and cybersecurity automation. Let’s build something meaningful.
